Impact of Attendance on Exam Performance by Attendance Level

This chart shows the percentage of students scoring 70 or above versus below 70, grouped by attendance level. Use the gender filter to compare student groups.

Key Insight

The chart indicates that attendance has a strong positive association with exam performance. Students with 90% or higher attendance are considerably more likely to score 70 or above compared with students whose attendance is below 90%. This pattern suggests that consistent attendance may play an important role in improving academic achievement.
